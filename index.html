About

Dark academia with kinetic curiosity.

I am a Taipei-based student exploring how policy, design, and narrative intersect. Outside the stacks, I follow rivers of research, coax old typewriters awake, and build prototypes for how better systems can listen to the people they serve.

Focus

Policy research, analog publishing, speculative futures.

Values

Elegance in craft, responsibility in knowledge, and honest curiosity.
